Barraza Picks up NL Monthly Pitching Honor
Wednesday, June 1st, 2022
Juan Barraza has been around the league long enough to know that relievers are often overlooked. That has never stopped him from doing what he does best -- and he did it extremely well in May and stole away with the National League Pitcher of the Month award.

The Braves bullpen ace collected 5 saves and 22 strikeouts in 24.1 innings, holding the opposition to a .221 average and posting a 3.33 ERA and a 4-0 record.

For the year Barraza has made 25 relief appearances and claimed 10 saves. His record is 4-0 with 39 strikeouts in 39 innings and a 2.77 ERA.
