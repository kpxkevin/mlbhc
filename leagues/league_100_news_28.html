Star Left Fielder's Son Starts at Exclusive School
Saturday, January 15th, 2022
Today was a big day for Theodore Gutierrez, son of the San Diego Padres right- hander, Jordan Gutierrez. It was his first day of kindergarten at the touted San Diego private academy, Winthrop Acres.

Theodore passed multiple aptitude exams in order to be accepted into the school, which has only 400 children over all 13 grades.

"Today is such a special moment for Theo and I am just so proud," said Gutierrez. "When I became a parent I never imagined the love and joy that he could bring me and I'm just so delighted to see Theo go to a school that can cultivate his academic desires."

This experience, coupled with the Gutierrez family's recent purchase of a luxury penthouse, show just how committed a left fielder the Padres have in Jordan Gutierrez.
