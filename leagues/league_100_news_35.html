Tinney to Give Up on Baseball, Pursue Hoop Dreams
Tuesday, March 15th, 2022
Justin Tinney has decided to quit baseball and pursue a basketball career. The Braves first baseman, who was a standout point guard in college, was drafted in the 2nd round of the pro basketball draft three years ago by New York, but chose to play baseball instead. Unfortunately, his career has stalled in the minors and Tinney says he's ready for another career change. "Baseball will always be my first love, but I just don't see myself having the same amount of success as I would playing basketball." According to pro basketball rules, Tinney is now considered a free agent and is therefore eligible to sign with any team.
