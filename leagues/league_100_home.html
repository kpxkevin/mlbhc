Mike Blanchard Donates Time and Money to Local Cancer Research Facility
Friday, May 27th, 2022
In a news conference today, Baltimore catcher Mike Blanchard has pledged to donate one-tenth of his 2022 salary to the American Cancer Society.

"I don't do it for the headlines; I do it for the patients and their families that are suffering through surgeries and treatment and recovery. Hopefully, one day, we can all find a cure. I'm happy to contribute to that goal, knowing that my money will be used for the greater good."
de Leon Razor-Sharp, Shuts Out Colorado
Wednesday, May 25th, 2022
San Diego Padres starter Salvatore de Leon tamed the Colorado Rockies 10-0 with a 6-hit shutout, finishing the game with 7 strikeouts and no walks in a sharp outing.

"I enjoy facing a team like that where I can get an out on one, two or three pitches," de Leon said. "It gives me a chance to be a lot more aggressive."

This season de Leon has worked 70.2 innings with 37 strikeouts en route to a 7-3 record and a 2.67 ERA.
