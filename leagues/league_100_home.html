Wright 5-for-6 for Chicago
Thursday, May 12th, 2022
It was a sensational day at the plate for Aaron Wright as he led the charge to a 17-5 win over the Phillies. The Chicago center fielder was pure misery for the Philadelphia pitching staff. He left them shell-shocked with 5 hits in 6 at-bats in an explosive display of hitting.

"Wright put the ball into play and he really hit it hard," said Rocket Can, manager for the Phillies, after the game. "When you do that, you've got a good chance to do some damage. And that's what happened. I feel like we threw some good pitches, but he just put some good swings on the ball."

Aaron Wright hit a solo-shot off Cruz Martinez in the 1st, hit a three-run double in the 2nd, grounded out in the 5th, singled in the 6th, hit an RBI single in the 7th and singled in the 9th.

For the year Wright has hit at a .358 clip with 36 RBIs, 7 home runs and 20 runs scored.

Orioles End Tirado's Streak
Wednesday, May 11th, 2022
Saverio Tirado's sizzling hitting streak was snapped at 22 games today as the Tampa Bay Rays dropped their contest with the Baltimore Orioles 3-2 at Oriole Park at Camden Yards. Tirado failed to get a hit, finishing 0-for-3.

After the game Tirado spoke with BNN and called his effort "quite a streak."

"I just ran into some good pitching today," he said.

This year Tirado is hitting .319 with 2 home runs and 17 RBIs in 30 games played. He also boasts an OBP of .459 with 30 walks and 21 runs scored.
