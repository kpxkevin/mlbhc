de Leon Superb Against Philadelphia
Friday, June 10th, 2022
Salvatore de Leon was throwing strikes early and getting in the count, his manager said after the San Diego Padres hurler tossed a 5-hit, complete game shutout against the Philadelphia Phillies.

"I went right after each hitter, trying to get ahead in the count," said de Leon. "I wanted them to swing the bats and keep my pitch count down. It worked. I felt strong the whole game, even in the last two innings."

de Leon walked 0 and struck out 5 in the 4-0 victory.

This year de Leon has worked 84.2 innings with 45 strikeouts en route to a 8-4 record and 2.66 ERA.

Great Stroke: Ledesma Gets 5 Hits
Wednesday, June 8th, 2022
It was a sensational day at the plate for Alex Ledesma as he led the charge to a 7-3 win over the Giants. The Tampa Bay left fielder was pure misery for the San Francisco pitching staff. He left them shell-shocked with 5 hits in 5 at-bats in an explosive display of hitting.

"Ledesma put the ball into play and he really hit it hard," said Jamar Quan, manager for the Giants, after the game. "When you do that, you've got a good chance to do some damage. And that's what happened. I feel like we threw some good pitches, but he just put some good swings on the ball."

Alex Ledesma singled in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, hit an RBI single in the 5th, doubled in the 8th and singled in the 9th.

This season in 53 games, Ledesma has hit at a .341 clip with 72 hits, 10 home runs, 44 RBIs and 39 runs scored.
