Hitting Streak Still Alive for Apgar
Wednesday, May 4th, 2022
Nobody has been able to shut down Baltimore Orioles second baseman Justin Apgar in recent days. The Los Angeles Angels fared no better than the rest.

Today at Angel Stadium of Anaheim, Apgar picked up 1 hit in 5 at-bats en route to a 12-1 Orioles victory. It stretched his hitting string to 20 consecutive games.
Justin Apgar grounded out in the 1st, flied out in the 3rd, was hit by a pitch in the 6th, hit an RBI double in the 6th, struck out in the 8th and flied out in the 9th.

Apgar is currently hitting at a .354 pace with 2 home runs and 18 RBIs.
