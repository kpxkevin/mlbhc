Vega Takes NL's Best Bat Trophy
Wednesday, June 1st, 2022
To survive in the bigs, you've got to be a scrapper like T.J. Vega. It never pays to take a day off from the batting cages or to shrug off the advice of the hitting coach. It all paid off today as San Francisco's left fielder pinned down the National League Batter of the Month trophy today.

He hit .350 in May collecting 12 home runs, 25 RBIs and 22 runs scored.

To date this season Vega is hitting .343 with 17 home runs.
