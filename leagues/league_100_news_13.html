San Francisco Giants, Phillies Agree to Trade
Saturday, January 1st, 2022
Both sides appear content with the trade finalized today that will send 27-year-old C Casey Alden to the Philadelphia Phillies, while the San Francisco Giants will receive 37-year-old C Josh Lutter.

"This is a move that filled a few gaps in our roster," said Jamar Quan, Giants general manager.

For his part, the Phillies GM said the trade was made "in the best interest of our organization's future." BNN analysts reported that on paper the trade seemed to be a plus for both ball clubs.
