Howard Wins NL Player of the Week Honors
Monday, June 6th, 2022
Brian Howard, known affectionately as "the old man" by the younger players in the Braves clubhouse, showed his teammates how to do it last week -- and in the process the 36-year-old vet beat out all competition for the National League Player of the Week Award.

He put up a .381 batting average, 8 hits in 21 at-bats, 3 home runs and 6 RBIs. Howard also scored 5 runs.

In 50 games this season Howard is batting .296 with 59 hits and 6 home runs. He has scored 31 runs and driven in 28.
