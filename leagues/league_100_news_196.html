White Sox End Apgar's Streak
Saturday, May 7th, 2022
The Chicago White Sox got more than the 4-2 victory at U.S. Cellular Field today -- they also put an end to the 21-game hitting streak of Baltimore Orioles second baseman Justin Apgar by holding him without a hit in 4 at-bats.

After the game, Apgar seemed more concerned with his team taking the loss than with the demise of his streak.

"I had some great looks at some pitches that I should have hit," he said. "I missed some that I shouldn't have swung at. All in all, I wasn't much help with the bat today."

For the year Apgar is batting .352 with 2 home runs.
