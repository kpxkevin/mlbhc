Wednesday, June 1st, 2022
Why is Saverio Tirado suddenly so popular around his hometown? Might it have something to do with his series of TV and radio commercials for a local car dealership that has taken the city by storm?

Viewers can't seem to pin down what exactly it is about the Tampa Bay player that is so endearing, but Tirado now has a cult following around town.
