AL Presents Batter of Month Trophy to Volpe
Wednesday, June 1st, 2022
Chad Volpe received the American League Batter of the Month trophy earlier today for his efforts at the plate this last month.

"He might be getting older," Baltimore's manager told reporters, "But he's been around long enough to know what it takes to put a stretch such as this together... and he doesn't keep that to himself. He's always talking to the younger guys in the clubhouse helping 'em any way they'll let him. Chad's value to our team goes farther than the numbers he puts up on the field."

To win the honor Volpe put up a .404 batting average, 15 home runs, 25 RBIs and scored 29 times in May.

In his 2022 season totals, Volpe has 21 home runs, .407 BA, .495 OBP, 47 RBIs and 49 runs scored.
