Cruz Zeroes in On Philadelphia
Thursday, June 2nd, 2022
Philadelphia dropped a 10-0 shutout to Luis Cruz and the Boston Red Sox at Fenway Park today. The Phillies offered little resistance to Cruz, who stopped them on 6 hits, fanned 6 and walked 4.

"It just felt right today," the left-handed Cruz told reporters after the win. "Even with the rain, it didn't throw me off my rhythm."

This year Cruz has posted a record of 5 wins and 4 losses with a 6.08 ERA. He has appeared in 11 games and struck out 36 in 60.2 innings.
