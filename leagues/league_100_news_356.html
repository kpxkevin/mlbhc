Perez May's Best Hurler in AL
Wednesday, June 1st, 2022
Vinny Perez nailed down May's American League Pitcher of the Month award today, adding pressure on Tampa Bay to get a deal done before the 24-year-old becomes a free agent.

Perez seems unfazed by the talk. "We get a deal done or we don't," he told reporters, "I'm not worrying about next year. Worrying about next year isn't what wins awards, you know?"

He fashioned a 4-1 record, reeled off 20 strikeouts, worked 31.2 innings and held batters to a .179 average for the month as he posted a 1.14 ERA.

Presently Perez has a 6-1 mark in 9 starts with a 3.29 ERA. He has given up 44 hits in 54.2 innings, struck out 35 and issued 16 walks. Opposing clubs have compiled a .217 batting average against him.
