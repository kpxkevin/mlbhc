Grado Files For Bankruptcy
Thursday, January 6th, 2022
Philadelphia starting pitcher, Tonito Grado, filed for bankruptcy this morning in a Philadelphia court. According to the Chapter 7 filing, Mr. Grado's restaurant chain lost $5.5 million last year. Among his assets, the starting pitcher listed over 2 million in Rolex watches.

Tonito has been quoted as saying, "What can I tell you? Those timepieces are the epitome of class. I can't say anything about my financial situation. Go through my lawyer."
