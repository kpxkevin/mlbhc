Martinez 5-for-5 for San Francisco
Friday, April 8th, 2022
San Francisco right fielder Daniel Martinez had a day to remember today, 5-for-5 against the Cincinnati Reds at Great American Ball Park as the Giants picked up a 13-6 victory.

"He was patient up there today," San Francisco skipper Jamar Quan told the press after the game. "I like the way he waited for his pitch and wasn't overly aggressive. Martinez has a tendency to chase bad balls sometimes."

Daniel Martinez hit an RBI triple in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, hit an RBI double in the 5th, walked in the 7th, singled in the 8th and doubled in the 9th.
