Orioles End Tirado's Streak
Wednesday, May 11th, 2022
Saverio Tirado's sizzling hitting streak was snapped at 22 games today as the Tampa Bay Rays dropped their contest with the Baltimore Orioles 3-2 at Oriole Park at Camden Yards. Tirado failed to get a hit, finishing 0-for-3.

After the game Tirado spoke with BNN and called his effort "quite a streak."

"I just ran into some good pitching today," he said.

This year Tirado is hitting .319 with 2 home runs and 17 RBIs in 30 games played. He also boasts an OBP of .459 with 30 walks and 21 runs scored.
