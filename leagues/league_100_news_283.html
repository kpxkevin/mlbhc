Player of the Week: Chicago's Dominguez
Monday, May 23rd, 2022
His solid numbers speak for themselves. That's why Javy Dominguez of the Chicago White Sox was named the American League Player of the Week.

For the week Dominguez batted .522 (12-for-23) with no home runs and 3 RBIs.

So far this season Dominguez is batting .396 with 3 home runs, 20 RBIs and 47 runs scored. He has 63 hits in 159 at-bats, 25 walks and a .487 OBP. Dominguez has played in 39 games.
