Quintana gets 5 RBI, Leads Astros to Win
The Houston Astros topped the Baltimore Orioles, 7-3, in American League action at Minute Maid Park. The player of the game was Houston third baseman Leo Quintana. Even with the win, the Astros have a dismal 13-30 record.

Quintana had a game-changing home run in the bottom of the sixth. On a 2-1 fastball from Ryan McCormack, he banged a grand slam home run to left, giving the Astros a 6-3 lead. It was the 4th home run of the year for Quintana.

"Being able to play a game for a living is one thing. Being able to play it well and contribute to a win is even better," Quintana told the Houston Observer.
